About Android Chapter

Android Chapter in association with DEV HUB, provides a platform for the students to inculcate the culture of Android App Development by getting trained in developing, debugging and deploying Android Apps.

The main aim of the Android Chapter is to provide Training and Resources so as to get certied by Google as Associate Android Developer
